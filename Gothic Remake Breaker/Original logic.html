FAQ

How do I solve lockpicking puzzles in Gothic Remake?

Enter the plate positions and interactions into the Gothic Remake lockpick solver. By default, the solver prioritizes reducing plate switches for easier in-game execution, but you can also use "Shortest moves" mode for the minimum move count.

Does this solver work for 7-plate locks?

Yes. The solver supports Gothic Remake lockpicking puzzles with 1 to 7 plates.

What do “Same” and “Opp.” mean?

“Same” means the affected plate moves in the same direction as the moved plate. “Opp.” means it moves in the opposite direction.

What does "x3" mean in the solution?

It means you should perform that move 3 times in a row. For example, "Plate 2 → Right x3" means move Plate 2 to the right 3 times.

What is the difference between algorithm modes?

“Fewer plate switches” tries to reduce how often you switch between plates, which can make the solution easier to follow, but it may use more moves. “Shortest moves” finds the solution with the lowest number of individual moves.

Can I share the solution with others?

Yes! Click "Copy solution" to copy the move sequence to your clipboard. You can paste it on Discord, Reddit, or Steam to share with others.

What if the solver takes too long?

The solver will try for up to 15 seconds. If no solution is found by then, it shows "No solution found" — this usually means the puzzle is impossible or too complex for the browser.

Is this an official Gothic Remake tool?

No. Gothic Remake Lockbreaker is a fan-made lockpicking puzzle solver.
